ABOUT US


Faizan Brothers (Builders & Contractors),is an organization of highly qualified engineers, project managers and technical professionals undertaking the most challenging and prestigious civil engineering and building construction projects in Pakistan.
INTRODUCTION
Faizan Brothers is one of Pakistan’s leading construction companies with over 3 decades of invaluable experience and expertise in construction and development of civil, steel fabrication and land development works. We carry out all aspects of construction and development projects, from planning to finish.
